Diversity Groups in Engineering

Our project aims to highlight the diverse voices within the engineering community, including but not limited to:

  • Women in Engineering
  • Black, Indigenous, and People of Color (BIPOC) Engineers
  • LGBTQ+ Engineers
  • Engineers with Disabilities
  • Veteran Engineers
  • First-Generation College Graduate Engineers
  • Engineers from Low Socioeconomic Backgrounds
  • Older Engineers who entered the field later in life
  • Engineers from Underrepresented Geographic Regions
  • Engineers practicing in fields with significant gender imbalances

Our goal is to bring forth the unique perspectives and experiences of these groups to foster a more inclusive and diverse engineering environment.
