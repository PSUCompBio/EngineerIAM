Engineering Diversity Interview Questions

As part of our initiative to highlight the voices of underrepresented groups in engineering, we ask each participant the following questions:

  1. What inspired you to pursue a career in engineering, and how has your background influenced your journey?
  2. Can you share a significant challenge you've encountered as an underrepresented individual in engineering and how you addressed it?
  3. What types of support or resources have been most valuable to you in your engineering career, and what do you wish was more readily available?
  4. How do you think your unique perspective as an underrepresented individual has impacted your work or the field of engineering as a whole?
  5. What advice would you give to young people from underrepresented groups who are considering a career in engineering?
